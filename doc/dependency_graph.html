Below is a dependency graph of the notes and tutorials in the course, respectively shown by blue rectangles and red hexagons. Hyperlinks will take you to the HTML version of each document; that should be fine for reading the notes, but you will want to load the tutorial notebooks in Jupyter in order to interact with them. The links among notes follow a natural progression for the course (left to right) rather than indicating every single conceptual dependence. Direct links between tutorials indicate that the results of one are required for the next (sometimes only for comparison purposes). Links from notes to tutorials indicate that the concepts in the notes are explored practically in the corresponding tutorial.
